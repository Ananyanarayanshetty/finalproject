Different type of Flowers

Flowers are the beautiful and colorful parts of a plant that usually bloom in different shapes and sizes. They come in many colors, like red, yellow, pink, and blue, and are often used to brighten up spaces, like homes and gardens. Flowers help plants grow by attracting bees and other insects, which spread pollen from one plant to another, helping them produce seeds..

Some flowers also have pleasant fragrances, which is why people use them in perfumes or as gifts to show love and appreciation.
